About the firm

Focused legal counsel with the range to support complex business needs.

Intellex Law Group is a Seattle and Mill Creek based business and intellectual property law practice serving a broad mix of founders, small businesses, creative professionals, institutions, and international clients.

The firm’s work spans brand protection, licensing, corporate governance, commercial negotiations, real estate matters, hospitality, international trade, and immigration. The through-line is practical legal counseling that protects value while helping clients move forward with clarity.

“Preserving, protecting, and ensuring exclusivity of ownership of intellectual property assets and rights at reasonable and affordable costs are what we do best.”
